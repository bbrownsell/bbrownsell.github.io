About Bad Boys

Bad Boys is a Corby 26 and was built in 2006 and has sail number GBR2603. She was formerly based on the river Humber.

The boat was purchased in September 2018 and is now owned by Peter and Mark Dale from the RTYC. She was laid up at Sandwich Marina for the Winter 2018/19. After the usual Winter maintenance she was put back in the water and then sailed down the River Stour to Ramsgate, where she is competing against the local IRC fleet in Class 2.
